Project: S.U.S.P.E.C.T

The Secret Universal Services Personal Entropia Connection Tool is a specialized desktop companion. It monitors your local game files to provide real-time data overlays and automated hunt logging.

Designed by hunters, for hunters. No more tabbing out to check your CSVs.

Tactical Interface

Keep your performance metrics in view at all times with the "Always-on-Top" HUD.

Acquisition

Download AlphaBeta v1.0.0

Target OS: Windows 10/11 | Format: .EXE Installer

Security Protocol

This software is currently in AlphaBeta and is unsigned. Windows SmartScreen will attempt to block the installation. This is a standard procedure for indie tools.

1. Open suspect_setup.exe.

2. On the "Windows protected your PC" screen, click "More Info".

3. Select "Run Anyway" to proceed with the installation.

Configuration

Linking the Data Stream:

  • Launch S.U.S.P.E.C.T from your desktop.
  • You will be prompted to select your inventory.csv after the app launches
  • You can download your inventory csv from entropiauniverse.com OR
  • feel free to click cancel and skip this for now.

  • I would recommend downloading it and selecting it so you dont get prompted everytime you launch the app; however, this is not required.

  • Navigate to the Dependancies Tab.
  • Select the Chatsnooper SubTab.
  • Set your ChatLog path (Usually: C:\Users\YourName\Documents\Entropia Universe\ChatLog.txt).
  • Ensure "Chat Logging" is enabled in your in-game Entropia options.
  • Navigate to the Settings Tab.
  • Set your FULL Ingame username
  • You should be ready to start tracking!
