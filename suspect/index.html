Secret Universal Services Personal Entropia Connection Tool

Welcome to the AlphaBeta release of S.U.S.P.E.C.T. This desktop application is designed to bridge the gap between your Entropia Universe logs and your gameplay data.

What does it do?

It "listens" to your local ChatLog.txt and extracts specific data in real-time. Whether you are tracking a long hunt or monitoring global events, S.U.S.P.E.C.T stays on top (literally) to keep you informed.

Download v1.0.0

Compatible with Windows 10/11

How to Install (The "AlphaBeta" Way)

Since we are in early development, the installer is currently "unsigned." Windows will try to protect you from this awesome tool. To proceed:

1. Run suspect_setup.exe.

2. When the blue "Windows protected your PC" screen appears, click "More Info".

3. Click the "Run Anyway" button that appears at the bottom.

Setup Instructions

  • Launch the app and wait for the Splash Screen to load.
  • Go to Settings and point the app to your Documents/Entropia Universe/ChatLog.txt folder.
  • Happy Hunting!
