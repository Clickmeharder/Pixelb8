A few words about:

C.o.S: Colonists on Stream or C.o.S is a collection of minigames intended to be played with stream viewers. The Mob Hunt: players in stream type !join or !play to try to kill a monster together for split loot (loot is divided amongst the surviving players based on damage dealt). Roulette: last Colonist standing wins game - colonists are placed in a circle around a loaded Capr pistol. The pistol shoots random players for a random amount between 0 and 10 damage. If a player’s health falls below 0 they are taken out and will not receive loot rewards. Deathmatch: this game has not yet been developed but this is the idea: Last man standing wins - similar to the roulette game except players shoot each other until only one remains. Team Deathmatch: !tdm !jointdm - also not yet developed but a similar concept to deathmatch, players will be randomly put into a team. The number of teams will be based on viewer count. or perhaps we will keep it simple and only have 2 teams. still undecided. however the winning team will split the reward. Lurkers: the idle game - still not developed but we hope to allow stream lurkers to lurk on the web for passive rewards like pixelated fruit, stones, and feces, and other potential random events. Once completed a player should be able to view their lurk logs on our webpage.
